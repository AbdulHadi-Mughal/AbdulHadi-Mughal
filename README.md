# Abdul Hadi Mughal
### Founder & Lead Architect at [Wattora Digital](https://wattora.digital)

I build high-performance digital infrastructure specifically engineered for the solar industry. My focus lies in eliminating the technical debt prevalent in renewable energy sales funnels by replacing legacy templates with **Edge-native, serverless architectures.**

---

## Engineering Philosophy

While my core product remains proprietary, my commitment to architectural excellence is public. I leverage a **Serverless Edge-First** approach to ensure responsiveness and global scalability.

* **Hybrid Intelligence:** I develop AI agents that bridge the gap between unstructured data (bill interrogation) and deterministic algorithms (precise ROI calculations).
* **Architectural Rigor:** Utilizing a structured monorepo to maintain a 95% shared codebase across client deployments, ensuring rapid feature parity and maintainable scaling.
* **Performance as a Feature:** By utilizing Cloudflare Workers and Neon-http, I eliminate cold starts and regional latency, a "local-first" experience for a global user base.
